Baran the Scarred

Description

Man of the South (Thief Leader) Broad-shouldered and imposing, Baran has a burn scar running down the left side of his face. His eyes are dark and sunken, and he limps slightly from an old war wound. He wears his sword like a badge of identity, even in places it draws unease. Survival has made him cruel, but there is a flicker of the man he once was. Friends with Kadran. See Baran's Lament.

Stat Block

Culture: Men of the South
Role: Desperate ex-soldier, thief leader
Attributes: Strength 5, Heart 1, Wits 2
Skills: Persuade 1, Athletics 2, Stealth 2, Battle 3
Combat Proficiencies: Sword 3, Dagger 2
Special Qualities: Driven by desperation; will fight or flee if exposed.

Current status

Captured in The Silent Hoofprints session. Currently working on probation in Bree, trying to earn his freedom and trust.
