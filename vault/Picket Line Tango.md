## Contract Overview

**Employer:** PURE Refinement, Inc. – Industrial Continuity Division  
**Point of Contact:** PURE Labor Recovery Desk

Following the deaths of two key personnel, **Norton Outpost** has fallen below required production benchmarks. Subsequent suspension of routine resupply has escalated local tensions into a full mining strike. PURE requires an external crew to enter the colony, restore operational order, and determine the source of ongoing instability.

Contractors will arrive by company transport at the outpost’s upper airlock and descend via a **3-hour space elevator** to the colony floor. Extraction is **not authorized** until the site is pacified and a final report is delivered.  
<br>

## Mission Objectives

### Primary Objectives
- Restore order at **Norton Outpost** and end the current labor stoppage.  
- Investigate the recent deaths of senior colony personnel.  
- Identify and neutralize any agitators, saboteurs, or other threats to continued operations.  
- Secure the colony’s critical infrastructure, including **communications**, **elevator access**, and mine output channels.  

### Secondary Objectives
- Assess the condition of colony personnel, supplies, and essential systems.  
- Produce a final incident report suitable for corporate review.  
- Preserve rhodium output capacity and prevent loss of company assets.  
<br>

## Compensation Package

#### Contract Payout
**300kcr** on successful pacification of the colony and restoration of operational control.

#### Personnel Loss Penalty
**10kcr** deducted from final payment for each dead colony worker or staff member.

#### Discretionary Bonus
Early contract completion may qualify for an additional corporate bounty.

#### Travel & Authority
PURE provides:
- Transport to and from the outpost.
- Temporary authority to **detain and interrogate colonists**.
- Authority to **enter any premises**.
- Authority to **seize property as evidence**.  
<br>

### Risks
- Ongoing labor unrest and anti-corporate hostility.
- Food scarcity and half-ration conditions throughout the colony.
- Vulnerable atmospheric dome and isolated crater settlement.
- Potential for organized resistance, sabotage, and further personnel deaths.
