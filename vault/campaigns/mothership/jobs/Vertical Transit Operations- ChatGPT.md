## Contract Overview

**Employer:** Welkin Orbital – Facilities Management Division  
**Contract ID:** 1-A9080-MF (Internal Use Only)

Congratulations on your assignment to **Welkin Campus Vertical Transit (WCVT)** Operations!  
You will join a select group of employees responsible for ensuring seamless movement of personnel, research samples, and classified materials across the orbital campus. WCVT is more than an elevator system—it’s the circulatory system of innovation.

> “When you move people, you move progress.”

Your vigilance ensures that transit between sectors remains safe, efficient, and compliant with Welkin’s operational protocols.

---

## Mission Objectives

### Primary Duties
- Monitor and maintain WCVT systems across research, production, and administrative sectors.  
- Log all transport events using **Form VTO-2** and submit incident reports via **Form VTO-3**.  
- Ensure passengers comply with **in-transit conduct** policies.  
- Protect clearance codes and biometric imprints against unauthorized use.

### Secondary Duties
- Document irregularities and performance variances (Forms L-9, A-12, IT-44, IT-21, or S-11).  
- Oversee onboarding briefings for new Vertical Transit personnel.  
- Respond to mid-transit anomalies or structural irregularities.  

**Special Directive:** Maintain silence and composure during unexpected pauses. Compliance is safety.

## Operational Hazards

While the WCVT system operates at peak efficiency, employees must remain alert to **Known Operating Irregularities (KOIs):**

- **Lighting Subsystem Overperformance:** Lumens may exceed threshold. Harmless but report via Form L-9.  
- **Auditory Loopback:** Automated announcements may repeat or desync. File Form A-12.  
- **Panel Inconsistencies:** Non-standard keypad responses. Cosmetic only; file Form IT-44.  
- **Transit Interruptions:** Rare, non-hazardous pauses. Wait calmly; file Form IT-21.  
- **Structural Irregularities:** Dust, rust, or vibration. Log via Form S-11—**Do NOT report to supervisor.**
