## Narrative stats  

Porto Bello, a Gau Spit-born Wordbearer. Edges in Sharps, Veils and Iron. +2 in Outwit, +1 in Concoct, Sense and Study. +3 in Low Sour and Gaudimm. +1 in Highvin. Aspects of Fengal Fortress, Camo Mottle and Docker Thread. Has salvage of ancient glass. Has a chart of many colors. Has a specimen of a heirloom green strawberries. Has a whisper of “safe and secure”. Has Drives of Deliver the message, find the Peni-Seal-In and locate cousin Lze-Kin. Has mires of leave a trail of spores, ravenous hunger for exotic food, and sad secrets from messages haunt his dreams.
