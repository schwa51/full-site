## Narrative stats  

Dapper Manits, an Ironbound Anchored Slinger. Edges in Sharps, Iron and Tides. +2 in Brace, Delve, Rattle and Scavenge. +3 in Low Sour and Signaling. Aspects include Ornate Cannon, Ghost-heart, tempered Anchor, and Barrage. Carries salvage of diffused shells and iron plates, has a chart of an old tradeway, and a whisper of “Back from Beyond”. Drives include Complete my Mission, Build the Biggest Cannon and Discover my Past. Mires are Betrayed by Someone I trust, Your material control varies erratically and weapons jam/misfire at bad times.
