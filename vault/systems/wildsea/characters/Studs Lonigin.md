## Narrative stats  

Studs Lonigin, a Tzelicrae Shankling Corsair. Edges in Teeth. +1 in Delve and Flourish. +3 in Low Sour and Knock. +1 in Chthonic, Gaudier and Old Hand. Aspects of Ersatz Limbs and Tremorsense. Has salvage of frayed ropes and patterned canvas. Has a chart of a port manifest in knock. Has specimens of spider husks and venom extract. Has whisper of “A prison of skin”. Has drives of Finding and handling shiny things and Demonstrating feats of strength. Has mires of high pitch noises distract you, everything tastes like portobello and sink into despair from all not accomplished.  

## What the crew knows About Studs Lonigin

Since many people fear Tzelicrae, most work hard to convince people they aren’t dangerous. Studs is over that and doesn’t care what people think anymore.
