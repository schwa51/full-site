## What This Is

Pure Transmission keeps a secret archive on **Benza-10**. They fill it with banned songs, radical music, and recordings they don’t want ordinary people hearing. Three of those tapes matter more than the rest.

They were made by political prisoners. Each one carries buried information about where those prisoners are being held now. We intend to find that prison. You are being hired to make sure those recordings leave the planet first.  
<br>

## What You Need To Do

### Primary Objectives
- Enter **Rosier Colony II** under forged work papers.
- Find the hidden **Hecate Cassette Archive**.
- Recover:
  - **Cassette A:** *Automatic Satisfiers – No Pretensions Accepted*
  - **Cassette B:** *Marika Kaltrejn’s Voyage In Cerdo*
  - **Cassette C:** *Hekatean Journey: Folk Songs and Folklore*
- Get the tapes back out without handing them to Pure Transmission.

### Secondary Objectives
- Learn anything useful about the prison, the Archive staff, or other restricted facilities.
- Identify anyone inside the colony who can be trusted.
- Do not burn future Survey routes unless you have to.  
<br>

## What We’re Giving You

- Forged work papers.
- A ride down through the atmospheric corridor on a company shuttle.
- Your real gear packed separately in rucksacks.
- Enough briefing to get you into the colony.

You are not being dropped on top of the target. The Archive’s location is secret. You will have to find it. Access is controlled, the company watches movement closely, and they do not like strangers asking the right questions.  
<br>

## What You’re Walking Into

- **Benza-10** is all snow, ice, darkness, and company control.
- Long-range communications are hard without Pure Transmission infrastructure.
- Rosier Colony II is a company town. Anyone without a role gets noticed.
- Travel off the roads is dangerous.
- The wilderness has **Tuskers**, storms, and enough cold to kill you before security does.  
<br>

## Payment

Enough to make the risk worth it. More if all three tapes come back intact. More again if the information on them leads somewhere real.

This is not a salvage claim or a corporate audit. This is a recovery mission against censors and jailers. If that matters to you, take the job. If it doesn’t, take it for the credits and stay out of our way.
