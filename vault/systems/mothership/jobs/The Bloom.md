## Contract Overview

**Employer:** Anonymous Shadow Client  
**Point of Contact:** Encrypted Black-Market Relay

Reliable contractors are required for a discreet recovery operation targeting **Outpost Choi**, a remote **Dyson Phytology** research installation on **Murandal**, at the edge of explored space. The site is divided across an orbital relay, a surface support base, and the primary research facility below the ice shelf.

Client intelligence indicates the target remains secure but isolated. Contractors are expected to infiltrate the site, acquire specified project materials, and deliver them to a prearranged dead-drop without drawing corporate attention.  
<br>

## Mission Objectives

### Primary Objectives
- Retrieve **Archived Research Files** related to Project **Bloom** from **Data Storage**.  
- Retrieve one **Processed Sample** from **Sample Testing** in **Choi Labs**.  
- Deliver both items intact to the agreed delivery point.

### Secondary Objectives
- Maintain operational discretion throughout the retrieval.
- Avoid alerting Dyson Phytology security or triggering sitewide lockdown.
- Preserve sample integrity during transport.  
<br>

## Compensation Package

#### Full Completion
**200kcr** upon successful delivery of both requested assets.

#### Partial Fulfillment
**50kcr** for recovery of either the archived files or the processed sample.

#### Penalty Clause
Failure to complete the contract within **two weeks**, or absconding with procured materials, will result in **blacklisting** and a **reparations bounty of 800kcr**.  
<br>

### Provided Assets
- Blueprint maps for **DeLange Station**, **Japline’s Ache**, and **Choi Labs**, including staffing estimates.
- General location and access briefing for the Murandal outpost.
- **Hi-Sec Access codes** for the Choi Labs access lift and other secured doors.  
<br>

### Risks
- Remote target at the edge of explored space with limited traffic and delayed support windows.
- Layered insertion route through orbital, surface, and sub-ice facility infrastructure.
- Armed on-site security personnel equipped for corporate asset protection.
- Extreme environmental conditions on Murandal, including radiation exposure and severe cold outside controlled installations.
